THE NEW YORK TIMES

Running to raise awareness of young sudden cardiac death

A cousin and a neighbor of a teenager who died suddenly five years ago from a condition known as young sudden cardiac death have raised more than 10,000 dollars in his memory by running half-marathons. John Smith died at the age of 18,leaving family and friends so shocked that they have only recently felt able to begin raising money for heart screening for 14 to 35 year olds through the Cardiac Risk in the Young Charity and raising awareness of the condition.
